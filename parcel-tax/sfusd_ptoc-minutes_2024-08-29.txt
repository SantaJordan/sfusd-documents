Meeting Record


1. Welcome
* Meeting began officially at 6:11 pm 

   2. Introductions 
   * Attendees (committee, staff)  provided a brief introduction and their connection to QTEA/FWEA 

      3. District Wide Updates 
      * Staff provide high level overview of the Resource Allocation Process and Budget Deficit:
      * Shared recent press releases connected to these two initiatives
      * Reviewed timeline for RAI from initial launch, town halls, CEQA/Equity Audits and planned announcement for 9/18/24
      * Reviewed the ongoing cost cutting including the Central Office restructuring and the plan for layoffs by the 25-26 school year.

         4. Review of Committee Roles and Ballot Measures
Committee reviewed the original language for the ballot measures, discussed the origin and basis for both committees, the core function (ensuring correct usage of funds) and other potential duties.


         5. Approval of minutes from January 2023 Meeting  
Minutes approved unanimously.




         6. Committee Future planning
         * Calendar has been set for three more meetings in the 24-25 school year:
         * Thursday, December 5, 2024 - 5 PM
         * Thursday, March 13, 2024 - 5 PM
         * Thursday, June 5, 2024 - 5 PM
         * Consensus from committee members to include many elements from prior years:
         * Audit review
         * Routine review of the QTEA and FWEA financials (including seeing context of 2 budget years for comparison)
         * Drafting report to the Board of Education (anticipate written report in lieu of presentation)
         * Agreed immediate priority is to increase members (including invitations to past members)


         7. Public Comment
         * No public comment

            8. Meeting adjourned at 6:58 pm
