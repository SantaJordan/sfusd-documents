Annual Enrollment Downloadable Files
Annual Enrollment Downloadable Files
Downloadable annual enrollment data for Census Day and Cumulative Enrollment disaggregated by student characteristics, including demographic and age range details.
Enrollment and Demographic Data
Census Day Enrollment Data
(
New
)
Downloadable data files containing annual Census Day enrollment disaggregated by grade, race/ethnicity, gender, student group, English language acquisition status (ELAS), and student age range.
Cumulative Enrollment Data
Downloadable data files containing annual Cumulative Enrollment data for the academic year disaggregated by  race/ethnicity, gender,  and  student group.
Historical Enrollment Data
Enrollment by School (1981–2022)
Historical downloadable data files of annual enrollment by school for the years 1981–82 through 2022–23.
Enrollment by County (1948–1980)
Historical downloadable data files of annual enrollment by county for the years 1948–49 through 1980–81.
Transitional Kindergarten Program Participation Enrollment Data (2013–2022)
Historical Downloadable data files of Transitional Kindergarten (TK)  program participation data for the years 2013–14 through 2022–23.
Note:
Pursuant to Education Code Section 60900(a)(4)(A), beginning in 2023–24, the California Department of Education (CDE) began collecting TK as a separate grade in California Longitudinal Pupil Achievement Data System (CALPADS), rather than as a program participation record which has now been retired. Moving forward, information about TK students is available in the
Census  Day Enrollment Data
file.
